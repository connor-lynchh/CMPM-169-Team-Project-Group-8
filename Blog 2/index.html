 I saw strange creatures in my Studio

                                                                                                         

          I recently experienced something I never thought I would. The details of this post are going to sound strange and will seem fictional but I don’t      care. I don’t care if any of you believe me, I just need to get this story out. Maybe find someone who has experienced the same.

        It started a few months ago when I moved into a little studio in the outskirts of the city. I’m a college student so money gets tight at times and      despite the studio being small I had to share it with another student. Although we lived a ways out from our college, life in the studio was generally      great. My roommate and I got along, we had an in apartment washer and dryer and we could play music as loud as we wanted given the closest       neighbor was about an 8 minute walk away. The only problem was that there was a minor cockroach infestation however, even this oddly disappeared within a few weeks of living there.

        After about a month living there, I began noticing markings on some of the furniture. All of them were lower than knee level and they seemed to resemble scratches. Neither my roommate or I owned pets so we figured one of us must’ve scratched them when moving a chair or the trash can.       Since we didn’t own the furniture this damage would come out of our deposit so me and my roommate started being more careful. Despite this, more       and more scratches appeared. I began suspecting my roommate      wasn’t taking the situation seriously and causing more scratches since I knew it wasn’t me. Before I could bring it up to him he mentioned something I did expect. He had found a small bite mark and the foot of the fake leather       couch. The marks resembled human teeth but the bite size itself was incredibly small. Almost like it was from a small baby with a full set of teeth. My roommate was the careless type and accused me of playing pranks on him. No matter how much I denied the allegation he was convinced.

        Eventually classes started getting tougher and I had to pull my first all nighter. My roommate had an easier class this quarter so he was fast       asleep. I was working until I noticed shuffling noises coming from the kitchen area. I was focused on my work and initially figured that it was my       roommate getting up to drink water. However, when the shuffling lasted longer than a few minutes I took a look over and realized he was still asleep. That's when I saw it. A small shadow darting across the small studio.
